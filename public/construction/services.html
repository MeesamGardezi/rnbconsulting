Our Services

Everything a rental property needs to stay in great shape — handled for you.

Our services are available as individual visits or bundled into ongoing maintenance plans. Whether you need a one-time fix or year-round support, we build around your property's actual needs.

01

General Repairs & Handyman

Small issues left unaddressed become expensive problems — and guests notice everything. Our handyman service takes care of the minor repairs that keep your property functional, safe, and looking its best.

  • Leaky faucets, running toilets, and minor plumbing fixes
  • Door and window hardware adjustments and repairs
  • Light fixture replacements and bulb changes
  • Drywall patching, caulking, and paint touch-ups
  • Cabinet, drawer, and furniture hardware fixes
  • Deck boards, railings, steps, and exterior woodwork

02

Lawn Care & Mowing

A well-kept exterior sets the first impression for every guest. We keep your lawn mowed, edges clean, and grounds tidy on a consistent schedule — without you needing to think about it.

  • Weekly or bi-weekly mowing on a fixed schedule
  • Edging along walkways, driveways, and beds
  • String trimming around fences, trees, and structures
  • Clipping and blowing of walkways and hard surfaces
  • Shrub and hedge trimming
  • Post-visit photo confirmation sent to you
Request This Service
03

Seasonal Yard Cleanup

Every season brings a new set of yard challenges. We handle the heavy work so your property stays safe, clean, and presentable throughout the year — not just when guests are arriving.

  • Spring cleanup: debris removal, bed clearing, and prep
  • Fall cleanup: leaf removal and lawn prep for winter
  • Storm debris pickup after wind or rain events
  • Branch and brush clearing from lawn and pathways
  • Gutter cleaning and downspout clearing
  • Outdoor furniture and deck winterization
Request This Service
04

Property Walkthroughs & Inspections

When you're not on-site, it's easy for small issues to go unnoticed until they become big ones. Our regular walkthroughs give you a clear picture of your property's condition before problems escalate.

  • Interior and exterior visual inspection
  • Written condition report with timestamped photos
  • Plumbing, HVAC, and appliance status check
  • Smoke detector, carbon monoxide, and safety checks
  • Pre-arrival and post-checkout walkthroughs
  • Flagging of issues with recommended next steps
Request This Service
05

Turnover & Refresh Services

The turnaround between guests is often the most time-sensitive window. We coordinate the minor touch-ups and prep work that keeps every stay starting fresh — on time, every time.

  • Post-checkout property walkthrough and condition check
  • Minor touch-up repairs between stays
  • Restocking of basic supplies (noted in advance)
  • Exterior quick-clean: sweep, blow out, debris pickup
  • Coordination with cleaning crews if needed
  • Ready-to-host confirmation photo report
Request This Service
06

Ongoing Maintenance Plans

The most cost-effective way to maintain a rental property is to stay ahead of issues — not react to them. Our maintenance plans give your property consistent attention on a schedule that works for you.

  • Monthly or quarterly visit packages
  • Bundled lawn care, repairs, and inspections
  • Priority scheduling for urgent requests
  • Seasonal service included at no extra charge
  • Monthly summary reports with photos and notes
  • Flexible plans — cancel or adjust anytime
