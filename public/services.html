Our Services

Comprehensive construction consulting solutions tailored to your needs

At R&B Construction Consulting, we offer a full suite of services designed to support every phase of your construction project. From initial estimation through project completion, our expert team is here to ensure your success.

01

Estimating

Accurate cost estimation is the foundation of every successful project. Our estimating services provide you with the confidence to bid competitively while protecting your margins.

  • Quantity takeoffs from drawings and specifications
  • Budget development and validation
  • Bid package preparation and support
  • Value engineering analysis
  • Subcontractor bid evaluation
Get Started
02

Scheduling & Planning

A well-crafted schedule is your roadmap to project success. We develop comprehensive schedules that account for every variable and keep your team aligned with project goals.

  • CPM baseline schedule development
  • 3-week and 6-week look-ahead schedules
  • Schedule updates and recovery planning
  • Delay analysis and time extension claims
  • Resource loading and optimization
Get Started
03

Operations Systems

Efficient operations are the backbone of successful construction companies. We help you implement systems that streamline communication, improve reporting, and enhance decision-making.

  • Custom reporting dashboards
  • Meeting cadence and agenda development
  • Document control procedures
  • Workflow automation consulting
  • Team communication optimization
Get Started
04

Project Controls

Protecting your project's budget and schedule requires vigilant monitoring and proactive management. Our project controls services give you the visibility and documentation you need.

  • Change order management
  • Cost tracking and forecasting
  • Project narrative documentation
  • Claims preparation and support
  • Earned value analysis
Get Started
05

Preconstruction Support

The decisions made before construction begins have the greatest impact on project success. We provide comprehensive preconstruction services to set your project up for success.

  • Constructability reviews
  • Trade coordination planning
  • Phasing and sequencing plans
  • Risk identification and mitigation
  • Site logistics planning
