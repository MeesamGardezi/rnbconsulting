07Design principles
01
Experience over theory
Every decision serves someone managing a real project under pressure. If a visual element doesn't reduce cognitive load or aid a decision — remove it.
02
Gold is the only accent
#b8860b and its tints carry all brand expression. Semantic colours (success, warning, danger, info) encode status — never use them decoratively.
03
Light and breathable
White and #f9fafb dominate. Dark surfaces appear only in hero and CTA sections. Generous spacing — construction data is dense, the UI shouldn't be.
04
Motion with purpose
GSAP ScrollTrigger drives entrance animations. One well-orchestrated page load beats scattered micro-interactions. Always respect prefers-reduced-motion.
05
Rounded but serious
r-md (8px) for interactive elements, r-lg (12px) for cards, r-xl (16px) for large containers. Approachable without being playful or consumer-grade.
06
Inter, nothing else
Inter at 700–800 for headings. 400 for body copy. Monospace only for financial figures, IDs, and percentages. No decorative or display typefaces.
